INSTALLATION INSTRUCTIONS
================================================================================

STEP 1: Extract Plugin
  Copy community_groups folder to /moodle/local/

STEP 2: Upgrade Moodle
  1. Log in as administrator
  2. Go to Site Administration > Notifications
  3. Click "Upgrade Moodle database now"

STEP 3: Configure
  1. Go to Site Administration > Users > Permissions > Define Roles
  2. Assign capabilities to appropriate roles

STEP 4: Verify
  1. Go to Site Administration > Plugins > Installed plugins
  2. Verify "Collaborative Spaces" appears in list

For detailed instructions, see INSTALL.md
